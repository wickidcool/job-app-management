Questions people actually ask.

Why isn't this just a fancier LinkedIn?

LinkedIn is a profile you put on display. Careerpin is the catalog underneath — the structured record of what you actually did, with the tech, the theme, the impact, the artifacts. It composes the right LinkedIn entries, resumes, review docs, and cover letters from your real material. LinkedIn is the marketing layer; Careerpin is the source of truth.

Will AI hallucinate things on my resume?

No. Careerpin is built around a "no content invention" rule. The AI helps you match the moment to material you authored — it never generates accomplishments. Every bullet on your tailored resume came from your own catalog.

Is this another AI resume builder?

No, it's the opposite. AI resume builders rewrite your resume from a job description and trust you not to notice when they make things up. Careerpin holds the structured record of what you actually did, and composes the right version of that record for the moment. The AI is constrained on purpose.

Where is my data stored?

On hosted Careerpin (app.careerpin.com), your data lives in our encrypted database. We don't sell it, share it, or use it to train models. On self-hosted Careerpin, your data lives on your own machine. Either way, export and delete are one click.

Do I need to be technical to use it?

No, if you use the hosted version at app.careerpin.com — sign up and go. Yes-ish, if you self-host — you'll need Docker and a terminal. Most people pick hosted.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, from your settings. You keep your data. You can come back later when you're searching again — your catalog will be waiting.
