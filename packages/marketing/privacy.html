Privacy

Your career data is yours for as long as your career lasts.

Our Approach

Careerpin stores your career history — accomplishments, salary expectations, rejection reasons, interview prep notes, and your inner narrative about your work. This is sensitive material. We treat it accordingly.

What We Collect (Hosted Version)

When you use app.careerpin.app, we store:

  • Your email address (for authentication)
  • Career data you enter (roles, accomplishments, applications, artifacts)
  • Usage data (feature usage, session duration — anonymized)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never see your full card number)

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data. Not to recruiters, not to data brokers, not to anyone.
  • We don't use your data to train AI models. When you use Careerpin's AI features, your data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing, but Anthropic does not train on your data per their Enterprise Data Protection policy.
  • We don't share your data with third parties except: Stripe (payment processing), Anthropic (AI features when you use them), and Cloudflare (infrastructure). All are SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • We don't track you across the web. No third-party analytics, no ad pixels, no fingerprinting.

Data Retention

Your data stays in Careerpin for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for product improvement.

Export & Portability

You can export your full catalog, applications, and artifacts at any time from your settings. Formats: JSON (machine-readable), PDF (human-readable). No lock-in.

Self-Hosted Careerpin

If you run Careerpin on your own machine:

  • Your data lives on your hard drive, in your PostgreSQL database
  • No data is sent to Wickidcool servers
  • No telemetry, no phone-home, no account required
  • If you use AI features with your own Claude API key, your data is sent to Anthropic per their terms — but still not to us

Security

Hosted Careerpin data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Database backups are encrypted. Access is logged and monitored. We follow industry-standard security practices and conduct regular security audits.

AI & Anthropic Claude

When you use AI-powered features (resume variants, job-fit analysis, cover letters), your career data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic processes this data to generate responses but does not use it to train models. See Anthropic's privacy policy for details.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you by email if we make material changes to this privacy policy. Last updated: 2026-06-09.
