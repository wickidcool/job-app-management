FAQ

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. Upgrade from Free to Pro anytime. Downgrade or pause Pro from your settings — you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What does "pause subscription" mean?

Most job searches last 60–120 days. When you're not actively searching, pause your Pro subscription. Your catalog stays intact. Resume Pro when you need it again — no data loss, no reactivation fee.

Can I export my data?

Yes, on all tiers. Your catalog exports to JSON and PDF. Resumes export to PDF and DOCX. You can leave Careerpin anytime with your full history.

Is there a team or enterprise plan?

Not currently. Careerpin is built for individual career management, not team hiring workflows.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. No annual lock-in — month-to-month only.

How does self-hosting work with AI features?

Self-hosted Careerpin supports AI features if you connect your own Claude API account. You'll need an Anthropic API key, which you can get from console.anthropic.com. Usage is billed directly by Anthropic at their standard rates.
