About Careerpin

Your career is a forty-year project. We built Careerpin to help you treat it like one.

Who We Are

Careerpin is built and operated by Wickidcool Software LLC. We're a small team that builds privacy-respecting software for people who care about the long arc of their work.

Why Careerpin Exists

Most career tools are built for the job search — the 60-to-120-day sprint when you're trying to land the next role. That's a real problem, and Careerpin solves it.

But the bigger problem is the 18 months before that sprint, when your career history quietly drifts out of date. When accomplishments scatter across Slack, Google Docs, and your memory. When the annual review document gets written at 11pm because the structured record doesn't exist.

Careerpin is the structured record. You capture your career once, in a searchable catalog of accomplishments — situation, task, action, result, tagged with the tech and the theme. Then you compose the right version of that record for whatever comes next: a review, a raise, a pivot, a search.

What Makes Us Different

Three things:

  1. We don't sell your data. Not to recruiters, not to data brokers, not to anyone. Your career history is yours.
  2. You can run Careerpin on your own machine. Same product, same features, your infrastructure. No cloud lock-in.
  3. AI helps you match the moment to your real story — it never invents the story. We constrain AI on purpose. No hallucinated bullets, no made-up accomplishments.

Our Commitment

Decade-long careers shouldn't live in vendor lock-in. Whether you use the hosted version or self-host, your data exports at any time. You can leave whenever you want, with your full history in hand.

We're building Careerpin for the long arc. That means sustainable business practices, no hype cycles, no growth-at-all-costs shortcuts. We charge for Pro features because that's how we stay independent and keep the product alive for the next twenty years of your career.
